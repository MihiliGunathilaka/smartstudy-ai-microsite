About Us

Built by Students, For Students

SmartStudy AI was born from a real problem — the overwhelming challenge of managing university workload effectively. We believe every student deserves a smarter way to study.

Our Story

From a Final-Year Project to a Mission

SmartStudy AI started as a final-year project at the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT), affiliated with the University of Westminster. As a computer science student managing multiple modules, coursework deadlines, and examination schedules simultaneously, I experienced firsthand the stress and disorganisation that comes with university life.

I noticed that existing study planners were either too basic — offering nothing more than simple to-do lists — or too complex for students to use consistently. None of them leveraged artificial intelligence to adapt to individual study habits and changing academic priorities. That gap inspired me to build SmartStudy AI: an intelligent, adaptive study planner designed specifically for university students.

What began as a software development project quickly evolved into a genuine mission to help students worldwide study more effectively, manage their time better, and reduce the academic stress that affects millions of learners every year.

The Problem

University students struggle with overlapping deadlines, poor time management, and exam stress — leading to last-minute studying and reduced performance.

The Solution

An AI-powered study planner that creates personalised timetables, tracks deadlines intelligently, and adapts revision plans based on individual performance patterns.

The Impact

Helping students reduce stress by 40%, improve productivity, and achieve better academic outcomes through data-driven study planning.
